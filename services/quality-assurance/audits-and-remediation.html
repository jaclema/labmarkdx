What are QA audits & remediation?

QA audits are a critical component of any Quality Management System (QMS). Internal audits and self-inspections ensure the performance and compliance of your organization, while supplier audits are essential for qualification and monitoring. QbD Group provides auditing services tailored to your specific needs, delivering detailed reports and actionable insights. Key aspects of our auditing services:

  • Internal audits: Comprehensive assessments to ensure your QMS meets regulatory standards.

  • Supplier audits: Qualification and monitoring of suppliers to ensure compliance and reliability.

  • Customized programs: Management of your entire audit plan, including individual audit reports and overall summaries.

  • Detailed reporting: Clear findings, non-conformities, and recommendations for corrective and preventive actions.

  • Follow-up support: Optional review of action plans to address audit findings

Why are QA audits important?

Audits are not just a regulatory requirement; they are opportunities for growth and improvement. Here’s why they matter:

  • Regulatory compliance: Meet expectations from authorities and certification bodies for regular audits.

  • Process optimization: Identify gaps or inefficiencies and develop corrective actions.

  • Risk mitigation: Reduce risks by proactively addressing non-conformities.

  • Continuous improvement: Use audits to refine and enhance your QMS.

  • Credibility: Demonstrate a commitment to quality and compliance to stakeholders.

Our process

1. Preparation

We begin with an initial consultation to define the audit objectives, scope, and criteria. This ensures that expectations are clear and aligned. At least one week before the audit, we share an agenda outlining the timeline and confirming whether the audit will take place on-site or remotely.

2. Audit execution

During the audit, our auditors thoroughly assess your procedures, records, and key personnel. Observations are documented in detail, and non-conformities are identified alongside actionable recommendations to address any gaps.

3. Reporting

After the audit, we deliver a comprehensive report that provides a full overview of the audit findings. This includes identified non-conformities, areas for improvement, and tailored recommendations to enhance compliance.

4. Follow-up

If requested, we assist with reviewing and commenting on corrective action plans to confirm their effectiveness and ensure that the proposed actions address the findings appropriately.
