Our services

At Labmark, we deliver a comprehensive range of life sciences services to support every phase of your product journey. Our national and international team of experts provides innovative solutions in quality, regulation, validation, clinical services, and more.
 
Discover our specialized offerings in regulatory affairs, validation, and services. Partner with us to achieve excellence and drive success throughout the entire product lifecycle.

Regulatory Affairs

Get your product to market with confidence through expert Regulatory Affairs support. From strategy to product approval, we offer tailored services to navigate complex requirements.

 

 

Quality Assurance

Development and implementation of Quality Management Systems (QMS) aligned with ISO 13485, WHO GMP, and other international standards.

Qualification & Validation

We ensure your products meet quality standards and regulatory requirements by qualifying and validating your equipment, facilities, and processes.

 

 

Clinical

Assistance in designing, managing, and monitoring clinical trials within Tanzania, ensuring ethical compliance with TMDA and National Institute for Medical Research (NIMR) requirements.
